    Frequently Asked Questions

    To get familiar with some stuff on the college grounds we had to make this FAQ page to show we know our way around. You can ignore this page if you want.

    You can find answers to common questions about the HZ below

    Click on a question to see its answer:

    How do you print a document from your laptop at the HZ?

    You need to add money to your student card and then find a printer on campus. After that, you need to scan your card and send the file you want to print to print.hz via your laptop.

    How do you scan a document and send it to your laptop at the HZ?

    To do this, you need your student card with money on it. Scan your card at the printer and use the screen to email the document to your laptop when it's done.

    How do I buy something on the HZ webshop?

    Go to the HZ website, log in with your account and search for a product. Add it to the shopping cart and you can pay.

    Where can I find the latest information and instructions about what to do if you are sick and have COVID?

    Go to the HZ website and search for the 'news' tab. There you will find a page about the coronavirus and the latest updates.

    How can you book a project room at the HZ?

    Go to the HZ website, log in with your account and search for the 'services' page. Click on 'Helpdesk & service automation'. You will be redirected to another page where you can click on 'Make a reservation' and thus reserve a room.

    What are the instructions about parking at the HZ parking lot?

    Go to the HZ website and click on 'About the HZ'. Then click on 'Locations' and the location of the school. On the newly opened page you will find the information about parking.
